Computer Buying Checklist

This website is going to showcase 4 different computers. 2 prebuilt computers and 2 pieced out Custom Built computers.

The first two are "Cheaper" computers that would be sutiable for a student going to school.

The last two are computers suited for professionals or people with more money than they know what do do with.


Cheap Pre-Built Computer

iPad Pro 12.9" iPad Pro Wi-Fi only, 256 GB SSD Storage, M1 Processor with 8 GB RAM, AppleCare+ for 24 Months, Apple Pencil, and Apple Magic Keyboard $1,962

The iPad Pro is a powerful computer that is an amazing device for Students. Allowing students to take notes by hand with the Apple Pencil or to type out an entire novel on the Magic Keyboard. The M1 processor has all the power and more that a student will need.
